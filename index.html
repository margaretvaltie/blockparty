Giddings Street Block Party

About the Party

Celebrate outside with your neighbors on Saturday, August 14th. We will have the street blocked off from 9am - 9pm for us to socialize, cook out, and mingle in the street. All are welcome. We hope everyone will attend and feel included. Don't forget to RSVP.

Help Out

This year we recommend all attendees get vaccinated. Those not eligible for the vaccine should wear masks and maintain social distancing. Bring a dish or games and let us know by Signing Up to Help.
