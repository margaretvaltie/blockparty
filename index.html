Giddings Street Block Party

About the Party

When: Saturday, August 14th 11am - 7pm

Where: 2200 Block of Giddings - from Oakley to Leavitt Streets

What: Meet your neighbors and enjoy a traffic-free street. Potluck style food, drinks, and grilling all day. Activities and fun for the kids. Please sign up to bring something!

Help Out

Is your pasta salad famous? Know a guy who can perform? Do you have a set of outdoor toys to share? We need help with set up, food to share, activities, grilling duties, and tear-down. Let us know by signing up to help.

Register to Attend

It's time to celebrate outside with your neighbors! We hope everyone attend and feel included. Help us plan food and activities with an accurate headcount. We are also collecting information about allergies and food preferences. Don't forget to RSVP.

COVID 19 Safety

Part of your Block Party planning includes a "COVID Captain." We will sign a COVID-19 safety affirmation form as part of the permit application. We recommend all attendees are vaccinated. Those not eligible for the vaccine, including children, should wear masks and maintain social distancing. We will ensure ready access to hand sanitizer throughout the day. The city will not allow Jumping Jacks or Bouncy Houses this year.
