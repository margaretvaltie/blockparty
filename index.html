Giddings Street Block Party

About the Party

When: Saturday, August 14th 12pm - 9pm
Where: 2200 Block of Giddings - from Oakley to Leavitt Streets. We will set up tables for dishes, bring out a grill, and coolers in the street.
What: Meet your neighbors and enjoy a traffic-free street. Potluck style food, drinks, and grilling all day. Activities and fun for the kids. No street parking or through traffic from 10am - 10pm.
Agenda:
11am: Street closed to traffic, set up starts
12pm: Grilling begins - hot dogs, burgers, or anything you bring
3pm: Organized activities for kids - hula hoop contest, bike parade, and more
5pm: Cheers to neighbors
10pm: Street reopens

Register to Attend

All are welcome: whether you rent or own, stay all day or just stop by. Help us plan food and activities with an accurate headcount. We are also collecting information about allergies and food preferences.

COVID 19 Safety

Part of your Block Party planning includes a "COVID Captain." We will sign a COVID-19 safety affirmation form as part of the permit application. We recommend all attendees are vaccinated. Those not eligible for the vaccine, including children, should wear masks and maintain social distancing. We will ensure ready access to hand sanitizer throughout the day. The city will not allow Jumping Jacks or Bouncy Houses this year.
